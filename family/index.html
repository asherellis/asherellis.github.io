My family is Jewish on both sides — roughly three-quarters Ashkenazi, one-quarter Sephardic. This tree was compiled with help from a middle school project, my zayde, Ancestry.com, Nathan Schachtman, and Claude — tracing six generations from the shtetls of Kupiskis, Timkovichi, Minsk, Smolyany, Grodno, and Salantai to the mellah of Casablanca, from Brooklyn, Detroit, and the Bay Area to Los Angeles.
